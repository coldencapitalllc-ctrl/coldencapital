How We Trade

The Colden Capital Process

Every trade begins with a macro thesis, validated through technicals and options flow before a single contract is placed.

  • 1

    Macro Thesis Formation

    Identify broader market catalysts — Fed policy, earnings cycles, sector weakness — that support a directional bias.

  • 2

    Technical Confirmation

    Chart structure, key levels, and momentum indicators confirm entry timing and validate risk/reward.

  • 3

    Options Flow Alignment

    Unusual options activity and dark pool data are screened to confirm institutional positioning aligns with the thesis.

  • 4

    Structured Entry & Alert

    Trade is sized, structured with defined risk, and a Telegram alert is sent with full details and targets.
