Who We Are

Not a fund.
A family enterprise.

Colden Capital is a family-owned private enterprise based in Fort Mill, South Carolina. We are not a registered investment advisor, broker-dealer, or financial institution of any kind.

We use the stock market and its instruments — primarily options and long-dated equity contracts — as a capital generation engine to fund tangible small business acquisitions and income-producing assets.

Our model is simple: disciplined market entries fund real-world opportunities. The goal is not to trade indefinitely — it's to build a portfolio of businesses and assets that generate lasting cash flow for the family.

From service businesses to equipment leasing to commercial real estate, every market position we take is a step toward something you can touch.

Discipline First

Every entry is rule-based and systematic. We don't chase — we wait for our criteria to align before deploying capital.

Market to Main Street

Profits from market positions are redeployed into brick-and-mortar assets: laundromats, equipment, leasing, and service businesses.

Generational Thinking

Every decision is made with the next generation in mind. We build infrastructure, not just portfolios.

How It Works

The Flywheel

I

Market Entry

Identify high-conviction setups using technical analysis, macro catalysts, and systematic criteria. Enter via long-dated options (LEAPs) with defined risk.

II

Capital Growth

Options leverage amplifies returns on correctly timed positions. Gains are tracked against asset acquisition targets.

III

Asset Acquisition

Profits are deployed into income-producing assets — laundromats, dump trucks, equipment — that generate reliable monthly cash flow.

IV

Reinvestment

Asset cash flow funds the next market position. The flywheel compounds over time, building equity across multiple verticals.
